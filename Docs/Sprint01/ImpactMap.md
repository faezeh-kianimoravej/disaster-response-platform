**Why (Goal)**

Improve coordination and response time during large-scale disasters
(floods, fires, accidents, etc.).

**Who (Actors)**

- **Citizens / Reporters**

- **Safety Region (Regio)**

- **Municipality (Gemeente)**

- **Emergency Teams (Police, Firefighters, Ambulance)**

- **Supporting Organizations (NGOs, Hospitals)**

**How (Impacts)**

- Citizens → can report incidents quickly and accurately.

- Regio → can validate and prioritize incidents.

- Gemeente → receives and manages assigned incidents.

- Emergency Teams → update the status of their operations in real time.

- Supporting Organizations → receive accurate information for
  coordination.

**What (Deliverables)**

- Mobile/Web app for citizens to report incidents.

- Central dashboard for Regio to validate and decide.

- Automated routing system to assign incidents to the correct Gemeente.

- Dashboards for emergency teams with real-time updates.

- Notification system to inform all stakeholders.

- Reporting & analytics for training and post-disaster evaluation.

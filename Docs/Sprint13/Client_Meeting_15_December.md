## Minutes

The team presented demos of the implemented and documented user stories, including integration testing, the design for responder crisis update submission, frontend mobile app architecture documentation, and log aggregation and monitoring documentation.

During the discussion about the MVP, Ivo clarified that there is no single MVP that needs to be fully completed and delivered at once. Instead, the expectation is that the team shows continuous progress every week.

Ivo also mentioned that he agrees with focusing more on the rubric requirements and testing during this quartile.

Regarding the Responder and Citizen applications, Sepideh suggested using a Progressive Web App (PWA) approach to simplify development and deployment. Ivo confirmed that using a PWA is acceptable and that there are no restrictions preventing this choice.

Finally, it was agreed that for the next meeting, the team will implement the quick action submission feature for responders.

# Definition of Ready

A user story is considered **Ready** when:

- The story is clearly described and understood by all team members.
- Acceptance criteria are defined and testable.
- All dependencies are identified and addressed.
- The story is estimated and sized appropriately.
- Required designs, mockups, or reference materials are available.
- The story is small enough to be completed within one sprint.
- There are no blockers preventing the team from starting work.

This ensures the team can confidently commit to delivering the story in the upcoming sprint.

# Client Meeting -- Agenda & Minutes

**Date:** September 15, 2025\
**Attendees:**

- Client: Ivo

- Project team: Sepideh, Faezeh, Furqan, Espen

## **Agenda**

1.  **Introduction & Objectives**

    - Discuss the project scope and main goals.

    - Identify primary and secondary users.

2.  **Disaster Management Overview**

    - Explain the current handling of disasters by "veiligheidsregio's".

    - Discuss pilot focus (Deventer) and potential for scaling.

3.  **Communication Requirements**

    - Reliable and secure communication between organizations.

    - Real-time data access for the public.

    - Strategies for areas without cell coverage.

    - Languages for communication (Dutch primary, English second).

4.  **System Functionality & Features**

    - Location tracking (automatic if possible) of important places and
      disaster areas.

    - Coordination between safety regions.

    - Visualization of disaster images.

    - Post-disaster data visualization for training or investigations.

    - Web and mobile application requirements.

5.  **Planning & Architecture Discussion**

    - Planning at Regio or Gemeente level.

    - Flow of information: how requests for help are routed (e.g.,
      Almelo → Hengelo or via Regio).

    - Alternative communication methods.

    - Department responsibilities and control by Gemeente.

6.  **Technical Considerations**

    - Choice of languages and frameworks.

## **Minutes / Key Points from Meeting**

- **Users:**

  - Primary: Organizations (fire department, police).

  - Secondary: General public.

- **Scope:**

  - System should handle multiple disaster types.

  - Focus on Deventer initially, but scalable
    nationwide/internationally.

- **Communication:**

  - Reliable and secure between organizations.

  - Real-time data to the public.

  - Provide options where cell coverage is unavailable.

- **Functionality:**

  - Location tracking for key places and disaster areas.

  - Coordination between regions.

  - Ability to show disaster images.

  - Post-disaster data visualization.

  - Public access via mobile, disaster teams via standard web.

- **Planning & Architecture:**

  - Incorporate Regio/Gemeente planning.

  - Clarify information flow and support requests between municipalities
    and regions.

  - Consider alternative communication channels.

  - Ensure clear roles and responsibilities of departments.
